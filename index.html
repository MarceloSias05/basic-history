Welcome to Basic History 4 You! Here you can find different topics about the basic information of history, like its definition, how it is divided, its origin, the categories used to study it, and the divisions of the sources of history. Our objective is to help young students that have problems with learning about history in school, we hope this website can help them understand the topics better.

If you want to travel to a topic, just click one of the buttons on the header of this webpage. If you want more information about the website and where to contact us, click ‘About Us’. Enjoy!
